Why we're here

Every ticket, every donation goes to the cure.

Garden Sessions benefits Touched by Type 1, an Orlando-area nonprofit that has stood alongside our T1D community since 2000, raising awareness, funding the search for a cure, and helping families with T1D thrive.

The glowing blue circle above the stage is the global symbol for diabetes. For one night, we let it light the music.

100%of proceeds donated

Donations are processed directly through Touched by Type 1's secure page, so your full contribution reaches the mission.
