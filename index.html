Why we're here

Every ticket, every donation goes to the cure.

Garden Sessions benefits Touched by Type 1, an Orlando-area nonprofit that since 2000 has worked to elevate awareness of Type 1 diabetes, fund the search for a cure, and help people with T1D thrive.

The glowing blue circle above the stage isn't just decoration — it's the global symbol for diabetes. For one night, it lights the music.

100%of proceeds donated

Donations are processed directly through Touched by Type 1's secure page, so your full contribution reaches the mission.
