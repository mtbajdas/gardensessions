Why we're here

Free to attend. Every donation goes to the cure.

Garden Sessions benefits Touched by Type 1, an Orlando-area nonprofit that has stood alongside our T1D community since 2000, raising awareness, funding the search for a cure, and helping families with T1D thrive.

100%of public donations to Touched by Type 1

Event costs are covered by our sponsors — so every dollar you donate goes directly to the mission, processed through Touched by Type 1's secure page.
